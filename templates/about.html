The Beginning

A passion for natural beauty

Nature by Regina was born from a simple belief: that the best skincare comes from the earth, not a laboratory. What began in Regina's kitchen — whipping shea butter and curing black soap by hand — has grown into a beloved natural skincare house.

Every formula honours the African botanical wisdom passed down through generations, elevated with a commitment to quality, transparency and care.
